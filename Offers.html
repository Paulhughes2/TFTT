Reptiles

Our reptile sitting services are available for one-off emergencies and for longer-term care. We can either come to your house and do some reptile-sitting, or we occasionally board them if they are housed correctly & easily transportable. As with all pet owners, those with reptiles want the best care for their pets—but they also face the unique challenge of finding others well versed in their pets’ specific needs. Fortunately, we are one of the few that provide professional care for reptiles and exotic pets. We offer regular visits for your pet, this includes heat, light and humidity checks, feeding and cleaning per your instructions. Regular customers include; snakes, lizards, dragons, skinks, monitors, iguanas, tortoises, terrapins, turtles and geckos.

Adventure Days

Our adventure program consists of a full day of fun in the country. We handpick each dog's day out based on your pup's personality and recall abilities. Your dog will spend his/her days out of the city. We take pride in giving our dogs great exercise while giving them the time of their lives out with their friends. We accommodate all dogs needs whether young, elder, small or large. We frequent beaches, trails and parks where dogs can truly be themselves. Previous days out have included the beach, Kinver edge, many of the national trust’s gardens, the woods and many more. The pups come home tired, fulfilled, and well behaved. We photograph every hike!
